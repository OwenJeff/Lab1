Capital Cites of the Four Corner States

About This Map

My map shows the precise locations and populations of the three capital cites of Colorado, Utah, and Arizona. The capital Phoenix, Arizona has the largest population by far at 1,445,362. Denver, Colorado comes in second at 649,495 in population. Salt Lake City, Utah is the smallest of these cities in relation to population at 186,440. The console log for this map displays the basic information for each of these three cities and runs in a fully functional "Loop".

Map authored by Owen Mickelson on November 3, 2016

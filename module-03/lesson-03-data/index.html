Kentucky Cities by Population

Lexington, Louisville, and Frankfort

About this map

This map was created to show the population characteristics of Lexington, Louisville, and Frankfort. These can be considered some of the largest cities in Kentucky. Louisville by far is the largest city in terms of population. Lexington is the second most populated in Kentucky. Frankfort in comparison of population is behind these other two cities, but it is the capital of the commonwealth of Kentucky. Overall the radius in and around these three cities has the highest population density in Kentucky.

Map authored by Owen Mickelson on October 30,2016

-The total population of these cities is: 1,100,041

-The average population of these cities is: 366,680.33

-The order of which these cities were created in the array: Lexington, Louisville, Frankfort
